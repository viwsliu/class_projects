Made a scene of a road with trees and a building on the side of road

Requirements for Asgn5:

3 primary shapes: Cube, Cylinder, Sphere. Cube is animated as is a light source (colored red).

Red cube is textured

2 custom 3d models: a scooter and the building

Camera moves with orbit controls

3 different light sources: red cube(red light), yellow sphere(yellow light), blue cylinder(blue light)

Skybox does not exist

There should be at least 20 shapes in this scene in total (trees and road included)
