Blueberry Jam

Simple, easy, and delicious

By Vincent and Tser
Difficulty

Easy

Cook time

30 min

Serving

15 Tablespoons

Vegetarian Condiment Side Dish

Ingredients

  • 9 cups of crushed Blueberries
  • 6 cups of Sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons of Lemon Juice

Cooking Instructions

Gather all the ingredients and combine it in a large saucepan.
Bring slowly to a boil stirring until sugar dissolves. Cook
rapidly til gelling point. As your mixture thickens, stir it
frequently to prevent sticking. Once thickened, remove from
heat. Skim any foam as necessary. Pour the hot jam into jars,
leaving 1/4 headspace. Place lids on jars. Process for 15
minutes in a boiling water canner.
