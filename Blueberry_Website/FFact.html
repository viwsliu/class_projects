  • Blueberry is ranked number one in antioxidant health benefits,
    in a comparison with more than 40 fresh fruits and vegetables.
  • Blueberry is one of the only foods that is truly naturally
    blue in colour.
  • The blueberry, Vaccinum, is one of the only commercially-
    available fruits that is native to North America.
  • North American indigenous people referred to blueberry as a
    “star fruit” due to its five pointed star shape blossom formed
    at the end of the fruit.
  • A single blueberry bush can produce 6,000 blueberries per year.
  • Blueberries consumption has been linked to health benefits
    including a reduced risk of cancer, increased insulin response,
    a reversal in age-related memory loss, and lowering blood
    pressure.
