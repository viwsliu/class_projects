Blueberry Muffins

A delicious muffin made from scrath with ingredients found around your house.

By Vincent and Tser
Difficulty

Easy

Cook time

1hr 20min

Serving

24 - 36

Vegetarian Breakfast Snack Dessert

Ingredients

  • 8 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons salt, preferably sea salt
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 1¼ cup canola oil
  • 4 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 4 cups buttermilk
  • 6½ cups blueberries, fresh or frozen

Cooking Instructions

First, gather the dry ingredients and filter it together.
Combine all the wet ingredients, except the blueberries,
and mix into the dry. Your batter should be thick, like
that of a frozen yogurt from a soft-serve machine. After
mixing the ingredients all together, add the blueberries
and fill your muffin pans above the rim of the cups. Bake
at 350°F for 35 minutes or until the muffins are golden
and set in the center. Allow the muffins to cool in the
pan for 30 minutes, before you dig in.
